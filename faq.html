Frequently asked questions:

How many flavors do you have?

We are proud to offer you between 20 and 30 delicious flavors at any given time. Our 50 plus flavors allow us to provide you with an ever changing selection of your favorites. View our current list of flavors.

Do you ever make new flavors?

Absolutely! Customers love to give us ideas and are always willing to be “taste testers”. New favorites in the past few years include: Strawberry-Kiwi, Raspberry Green Tea, Coffee Caramel, Mango and many others.

How often do your flavours change?

Every month! We rotate flavours based on availability of ingredients and post the week’s offerings on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

Do you have any fat free or low-fat options?

Of course! We offer a line of low-fat options as well as waffle ice-cream.

Do you deliver?

We would love to serve you by delivering during our opearting hours (11:00 AM to 7:00 PM) Monday through Friday for orders over $20.

What’s mission for Diazepam Cafe?

Our mission is to make, innovate the finest and best dairy ice-cream with a commitment to incorporate wholesome, natural ingredients for great ice-cream lovers.
